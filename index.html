About

Hi, I'm Joryn.

Educational Journey

I studied for three semesters at Algonquin College, taking courses in their Interactive Media Design program. Nearing the end of my diploma, I decided I wanted to get a degree in Information Technology. After being accepted into the BIT Interactive Multimedia Design program, I transitioned my studies to Carleton University.

Extra-curricular

While studying at Carleton University, I have connected with the community through student organisations and volunteer positions, including:

  • &Design

    First Year Rep

    V-P Publications

    This society represents all design majors in Carleton's Faculty of Engineering and Design. In my first year, I was elected the BIT First Year Representative. During that time I helped increase first year BIT involvement in &Design, and hosted multiple events. For my second year I was elected Vice-President Publications. I look forward to begin my publications responsibilities in fall of 2026.

  • BITSoc

    IMD Representative

    The BIT Society represents each stream in Carleton's Bachelor of Information Technology. For my second year, I have been elected the IMD Representative. I will be advocating for my program and its members, as well as helping other IMD students understand the program and engage with their peers.

  • Carleton Anti AI Society

    Founder

    President

    In my first year, I founded the Carleton Anti AI Society to stand against the use of generative AI at Carleton University. I will be serving as president starting in the fall of 2026, with responsibilities such as: leading other CAAS executives, hiring directors, and planning society actions and events.
